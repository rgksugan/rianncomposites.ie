About Riann Composites

Specialists in fiberglass & carbon fiber repair

Replacing a damaged composite panel, hull or body part is expensive — and often completely unnecessary. At Riann Composites we restore the strength, finish and structure of fiberglass (GRP) and carbon fiber components so they perform like new, for a fraction of the cost of replacement.

From cracked camper roofs and boat hulls to carbon performance parts and industrial roofing, we deliver professional, documented repairs backed by an honest assessment and a one-year structural warranty.
