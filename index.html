About Riann Composites

Built from hands-on craftsmanship

Riann Composites was founded by Antony Milton Raja, a hands-on composite technician with a background in aviation repair, sheet-metal work, fibreglass, carbon fibre, bonding and fabrication.

Antony began his career in aviation working with sheet metal — bending, riveting and shaping aircraft panels. His interest in composites started when he repaired a panel using resin and fibreglass. The moment he shaped a strong part from raw materials by hand, he knew this was the craft he wanted to build his future around.

His first major composite project was a boat — designed abroad but brought to life through careful hands-on fabrication. That project showed him what composite materials can do when skill, patience and the right process are used together.

Today, Antony brings that same care and precision to campervans, boats, farm machinery, vehicle plastic panels and custom composite parts.

How It Works

A simple repair process

  1. 1

    Send Photos

    WhatsApp or email clear photos: close-ups of the damage, the full part, any broken pieces, the model, approximate size and your location.

  2. 2

    Initial Repair Opinion

    We review the photos and give an honest first opinion — whether it looks repairable, replacement may be better, or a workshop inspection is needed.

  3. 3

    Workshop Assessment

    Suitable jobs come to the workshop by appointment. We check material type, crack direction, missing pieces, previous repairs and backside access.

  4. 4

    Clear Quote First

    You get a clear quote before work begins. For custom work, special materials or larger jobs, a deposit may be required before materials are ordered.

  5. 5

    Repair & Reinforcement

    We use the correct method — fibreglass or carbon repair, epoxy bonding, plastic welding, hot-staple, stainless mesh backing, panel rebuilding, sanding and finishing.

  6. 6

    Before & After

    Where suitable, we provide before-and-after photos and a short repair note explaining what was repaired — keeping the process transparent.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Can every damaged part be repaired?

No. Some parts are better replaced. We inspect the damage and give honest advice before work starts.

Do you repair tractor plastic panels?

Yes, depending on the plastic type and damage. We repair selected tractor, machinery and vehicle plastic panels using plastic welding, hot staples, reinforcement mesh and flexible finishing methods where suitable.

Do you repair campervans and caravans?

Yes. We repair selected GRP and fibreglass damage on campervans, motorhomes and caravans, including cracks, holes, corner damage, roof edge damage and panels.

Do you repair boats?

Yes. We repair selected fibreglass and GRP marine parts. Large structural hull repairs require inspection before acceptance.

Do you make custom parts?

Yes. We can make small custom fibreglass or carbon fibre parts, covers, guards, panels, mouldings and prototypes.

Do you repair carbon fibre?

Yes, selected carbon fibre parts can be repaired or refinished. Safety-critical parts may be refused if repair is not suitable.

Do you repair aircraft parts?

No. Riann Composites does not provide aircraft repair services. Our services are for non-aviation fibreglass, carbon fibre and plastic repair work.

Do I need to bring the part to you?

Most repairs are workshop-based and by appointment only. Mobile inspection or collection may be discussed depending on the job and location.

Do you paint after repair?

Finishing and paint preparation can be discussed. Full paint matching may be arranged case by case depending on the job.
